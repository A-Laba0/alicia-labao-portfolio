# Portfolio Design Direction

## Palette
- Primary: #1A2238 (deep navy, for headlines, nav, and dark sections)
- Accent: #FFC857 (warm gold, used sparingly on CTAs and highlights)
- Background: #F7F9FB (soft off-white, slightly cool)
- Text: #1F2937 (dark slate, for body copy)

## Typography
- Display (headlines): Plus Jakarta Sans (700/800 weight)
- Body: Plus Jakarta Sans (400 + 500 weight)

## Visual language
Clean editorial-professional. Generous whitespace, strong type hierarchy, and a calm navy/off-white base with gold used only as a small accent. A small, subtle portrait near the top (not a giant hero photo). Work shown as a tidy grid of project tiles with light cards and soft shadows. Thin hairline dividers between sections, minimal boxes, no gradients. Modern, trustworthy, uncluttered.

## Tone
Tagline: "I turn complex processes into streamlined, AI-powered workflows."

## Voice
Confident-direct, but warm. Short, clear sentences. First person. No buzzwords or fluff. Speaks like a capable professional who gets things done and knows when to ask the right questions.
